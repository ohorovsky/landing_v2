The Process

Keep an eye on your groups

Track keywords and links. Measure engagement and activity.

Keywords

Our facebook group statistics are based on keywords that reveal a spam degree, personality estimations and promotional affinities.

Links

Find out whether active members are spamming or posting automated messages and tailor your content strategy accordingly.

Reactions and comments

Reactions, comments and shares will help you decide how and with what frequency you should interact with the facebook group in order to blend in.

Number of (active) members

These facebook group statistics will help you estimate the reach that your campaign should have and the potential of a particular group.

Your Strategy

Search and filter results

Search for public groups that you’re not a member of. Search and filter posts according to your keywords of interest.

Post Feed

Analyse the content in any public facebook group in a separate feed within the dashboard. See top posts and comments, get copywriting and imagery advice and identify the posting style of the facebook group.

Public group

Search for and browse any public group that may be of interest.

Keywords

Search for keywords that define your area of interest. Filter your search and choose which groups to get results from.

Get Notified

Let Facebucket poke you

Receive notifications about the people and keywords that you wish to monitor.

Keywords

Get in-app, e-mail or SMS notifications when keywords are used in posts within the groups you’ve chosen to monitor.

People

Get in-app, e-mail or SMS notifications when the members you’re following are engaging in posting activities in groups.
